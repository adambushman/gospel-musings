The Basics

The church furthers our earthly mission and purpose:

Demonstrate faithfulness in the gospel by learning & gaining mortal experiences

We can narrow this down, highlight certain components, but that’s it.

Paul writing to a branch of the primitive Church in Ephesus:

Till we all come in the unity of the faith, and of the knowledge of the Son of God, unto a perfect man, unto the measure of the stature of the fulness of Christ:

New Testament | Ephesians 4:13

The Church handbook leads off with this same mission:

Jesus Christ established His Church to enable individuals and families to do the work of salvation and exaltation.

General Handbook 1.3

Observations:

  • The church doesn’t execute the work of salvation and exaltation
    • Individuals and families operate in the environment setup by the church
  • All stems back to Jesus Christ
    • Center of the Plan of Salvation
    • The flywheel to the work
    • We choose our path; Christ perfects us along the way

Church is the environment for salvation and exaltation.

There are ingredients to this recipe that yields the best results for the most Saints.

The Recipe

To help accomplish this divine purpose, the Church and its leaders provide:

  • Priesthood authority and keys.
  • Covenants and ordinances.
  • Prophetic direction.
  • Scriptures.
  • Gospel learning and teaching support.
  • Service and leadership opportunities.
  • A community of Saints.
